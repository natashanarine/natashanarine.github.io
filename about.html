About Me

I’m Natasha Narine, a first-generation student studying Computer Information Systems with an App Development concentration at Georgia State University.This interdisciplinary track enables me to combine my strong creative background with technical skills to solve real problems and bring ideas to life through solutions that are functional, intuitive, and visually engaging.

I’m passionate about taking ideas from concept to execution, whether it’s an app, campus event, or craft. I find the most fulfillment in creating experiences that bring people together, and I thrive in environments where curiosity, adaptability, and thoughtful problem-solving drive meaningful results. Above all, I aim to make technology approachable, enjoyable, and purposeful, blending precision with personality in every project I take on.
